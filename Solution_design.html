Product Design Criteria

Criteria Details
Customer Design The sleeve must be able to fit the natural shape of the wrist and forearm to provide optimal support and comfort during use.
Performance Should be able to support the wrist and its functions and work to alleviate any pain it may experience.
Target Cost The item should be affordable to everyone, so that those of lower income levels who require it can afford it.
Materials Nylon used as the main material for its cheap, durable, and lightweight properties.
Safety, Legal, and Ethical Issues Potential for allergic reactions or rashes from the material.
Durability Should be able to stretch without tearing and maintain functionality over a long period of time.
Operating Environment The item should be easy to clean in case of dirt without losing its functionality and should withstand wear and tear from many uses.
Ergonomics Include adjustable straps or fastenings to allow users to customize the fit and level of support as needed.
Easy Maintenance Ensure the sleeve is machine washable or easy to clean to maintain hygiene without damaging the material.
Aesthetic Appeal Offer a range of colors and designs to make the sleeve visually appealing and encourage user adoption.
